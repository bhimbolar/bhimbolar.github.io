Data Science Career Track

The course you need to learn problem formulation, identification of the right datasets, data wrangling, modelling, data visualization and how to effectively communicate insights.
Details

Requirements

  • Have a computer with Internet connection.
  • Be ready to learn an insane amount of awesome stuff.
  • Prepare to analyze real data.

Description

Data Science is one of the most future-proof and highly demanded career opportunities in recent history. Our programme is specially designed to help participants launch a career in data science through hands-on experience by working on real problems, 1-on-1 mentorship from proven industry experts, specially curated curriculum and career coaching for making the right career moves.
During this programme, participants will work on problem formulation, identification of the right datasets, data wrangling, modelling, data visualization and how to effectively communicate insights. This will be achieved through a combination of carefully curated python-based programming, simplified and just-enough mathematics/statistics and machine learning syllabus. We will not focus on theories, rather, efforts will be concentrated on solving real life problems, particularly those that confront us on a daily basis. Furthermore, participants will develop a portfolio of evidence that can be used to demonstrate their knowledge.

Prerequisites

  • Critical Thinking.
  • Minimum of Bachelor's Degree/Higher Diploma in Sciences, Engineering and Social Sciences.
  • Good foundation in a programming language (preferably python).

Syllabus

  • Providing Data Driven Solutions
  • Python for Data Analyst
  • Introduction to SQL
  • Capstone 1
  • Career Coaching Check-In
  • Intermediate SQL
  • Basic Statistics.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis
  • Data Visualization with Tableau and PowerBI
  • Communicating Outcomes
  • Capstone 2
  • Career Coaching Check-In
