Career Coaching

Hands-On Practice

Our mentors are seasoned data scientist that are committed to your success. You will have 1-on-1 access to a carefully selected mentor that will provide guidance on our carefully developed curriculum, support in choosing the right capstone project and entering the industry. The goal of our mentorship program is to:

  • Help you stay motivated through the program.
  • Eliminate doubts and answer questions during the process.
  • Develop the right portfolio and brand required to launch your career.
  • Provide constructive and tailored feedback.
  • Help you build the right network.

Perception is everything! Our research and experience indicate that developing the right technical skill is not enough to break into the industry, land your dream job or launch your idea into a successful start-up. After developing the required technical skills, then comes the problem of getting the interview, which is where many people get frustrated and will likely start second-guessing themselves or feel they are not good enough. Career coaching is an integral part of all our programme with the goal of helping each participant develop a strong brand to put their best foot forward when they step into the market. Our career coaches will help you navigate the job market issues such as reviewing your resume, networking and preparing for interviews We will focus on:

  • Developing your personal brand.
  • Understanding and highlighting your strengths.
  • Building your resume.
  • How to get interviews.
  • Preparing for Data Science/Analytics interviews.
  • Salary Negotiation.

DataCquence community is made up of students, mentors and advisor that you will be able to network with or get your questions answered. Each participant is encouraged to choose capstone projects that align with their brand and career objectives. Working on the right real life project helps participants to develop the necessary skills needed for the daily life of a data scientist/Analyst and develop experiential knowledge required to be successful in interviews.

The mentors will guide participants in framing the right problems, data collection, cleaning and analysis. Delivering insights from data analytics to diverse audiences is a critical part of being a data scientist or analyst. Our participants work with their mentors to present their findings to an audience of peers, mentors and investors where they will be able to develop the necessary presentation skills.
