About DataCquence

Background

Discover what drives us and makes us tick at DataCquence

Our mentors are Data Scientists and Analytics professionals with between 5 and 20 years of experience across industries such as Banking, Healthcare, Oil and gas, Consulting and High Tech. All of them have been privileged to support initiatives at large organizations such as Facebook, Chevron, Deloitte Consulting, etc. As a result, the have also helped in mentoring many people in their organizations and DataCquence was established to bring the same experience to you.
Data has been identified as the new oil and even as the fourth factor or production by some economist. Therefore, a career in data science and analytic or becoming a data analytics savvy professional is not just a nice to have but preparing your career for the future. Whether you are just starting out or thinking of making the right career move, our programs are specially designed to help you launch out and give you an edge in the market.
Let’s face it! There are lots and lots of resources on the internet for anyone interested in data science, machine learning etc and of course google.com is your friend if you will be successful. However, the information can be extremely overwhelming! Hence, our focus is to provide a blended one-to-one mentoring bootcamp approach virtually from the comfort of your home. Moreover, you will put acquired skills to practice by solving real life problems with the guidance of your mentor and you will also be working with a career coach that will get you ready for your next role.
